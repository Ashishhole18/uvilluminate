F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long does COVID-19 Live on Surfaces?

    6 hours - 9 days on surfaces

  • What is the difference between a virus and bacteria?

    Bacteria are self-contained, have cell walls, and can survive and replicate on their own. Viruses are DNA molecules that may be naked or encapsulated and require a host to replicate. They cannot be treated with antibiotics and require a vaccine.

  • What is the biggest issue with this COVID-19 virus?

    High secondary infection rate, Rapid spread (more rapid than SARS or MERS), Fatality rate (2-3%).

  • Is ultraviolet light effective against COVID-19?

    Ultraviolet light destroys DNA of viruses, bacteria, and fungi.
